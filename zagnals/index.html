ZAGNALS™

A professional full-screen display board for iOS. Built for stages, boardrooms, classrooms, and anywhere a screen needs to command a room at distance.

Download on the App Store   Privacy Policy

Three displays. Zero distractions.

ZAGNALS fills the screen with exactly what the room needs to see — nothing more.

  • CUE — Full-screen message board. Type your cue, pick white or amber text, set a duration. One tap launches it full-screen.
  • Clock — Large-format clock readable across a room. 12h or 24h, three color modes: dark, light, or alternating.
  • Countdown — Precision countdown timer with configurable audio, flash, and haptic alerts. Siri control. Show Clock overlay.

Built for professionals.

  • Maximum brightness, full-screen, landscape-locked — the display is the device
  • Shake or long-press to exit — no visible controls during display
  • Flash alerts: hard-cut white inversion with haptic feedback
  • Siri control for countdown (start, pause, resume, restart)
  • Readable at 3 meters / 10 feet on any supported iPhone or iPad

Completely private.

ZAGNALS is fully offline. No network access. No accounts. No analytics. No ads. Your data never leaves your device. Read the full privacy policy →

Requirements

  • iOS 16.0 or later
  • iPhone or iPad
  • No internet connection required — ever
